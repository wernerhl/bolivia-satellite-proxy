## Unexpected findings

1. **VIIRS elasticity is significant but lower than HSW 2012.**
   $\hat\beta = 0.074$ (p = 0.04), vs HSW's ~0.30. Short panel
   (2013–2016, 1990-base ends in 2016). Claude: flag as consistent
   with Gibson et al. (2021) single-country attenuation.

2. **NDVI elasticity inference is unreliable.** Point estimate 0.169
   with n = 12; two-way clustering on 5 × 3 clusters is degenerate.
   Recommend paper prose reports the point estimate and explicitly
   defers inference to R&R when additional annual agricultural-GVA
   observations accumulate.

3. **1990-base quarterly GDP ends at 2024Q4.** The 2017-base series is
   not public as of 2026-04-24. Elasticities are invariant but
   absolute-level comparisons require rebasing. Worth a footnote in
   §3.2.
